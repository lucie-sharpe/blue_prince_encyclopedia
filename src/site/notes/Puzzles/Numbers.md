- **Blue:** Add
- **Yellow**: Subtract
- **Pink:** Multiply
- **Purple:** Divide

- **Square shape:** Square the value after finishing the stage indicated by colour of the square

## Dartboard
- Work from the centre outwards
	- Add -> Sub -> Mul -> Div
	- Apply square after stage indicated by colour
